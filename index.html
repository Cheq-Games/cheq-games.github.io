Welcome to Cheq Games. We are a game company that produces games for the masses' enjoyment and satisfaction. We are in teams;
  • The Programming team, which is a team that are obsessed with the coding needs of the game development.
  • The Design team which is a team of the game development, they make models, weapons, and other cool stuff for the game.
  • The Story Board Team is another team, their job and focus is to make up the story line of the game, and also to make scripts of the game.
  • And the last team is the Audio Team, as their name says, they make the sounds of the game to enhance gaming experience.
